Support

Help with Whatifolio.

If something looks wrong, start by restoring purchases, checking your network connection, and sharing your in-app Support ID when asking for help.

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Contact support

Open a support request on GitHub: github.com/whatifolio/whatifolio_legal/issues.

Please include your device type, app version if available, what you expected to happen, what happened instead, and your in-app Support ID if the issue is related to purchases, sign-in, whatifs, or Lifetime Unlimited.

Restore purchases

If Lifetime Unlimited or a recent whatif purchase does not appear after checkout, reinstalling the app, or switching devices, open the paywall and choose Restore purchase. Purchases are restored through the App Store or Google Play account used at checkout. Optional sign-in with Apple or Google can also help keep purchases and whatifs associated with the same Whatifolio account across supported devices.

Delete account

If you signed in with Apple or Google, open the app's Account & purchases section and choose Delete account. This removes the Whatifolio account link, active sessions, wallet records, and app account data from our backend. Store purchase history remains managed by Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat.

Billing, refunds, and purchase issues

Whatifolio currently offers consumable whatif packs and a non-renewing Lifetime Unlimited purchase. Payments, refunds, taxes, regional pricing, and store receipts are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play according to their policies.

Welcome whatifs and calculations

New users may receive welcome whatifs. Standard calculations spend one whatif each. You can buy additional whatifs in packs, or unlock Lifetime Unlimited to calculate without spending whatifs. The app may use anonymous account, device, and network-based controls to reduce abuse.
