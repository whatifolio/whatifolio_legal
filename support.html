Support

Help with Whatifolio.

If something looks wrong, start by restoring purchases, checking your network connection, and sharing your in-app Support ID when asking for help.

Effective date: May 13, 2026

Contact support

Open a support request on GitHub: github.com/vitaliiusatenko/whatifolio_legal/issues.

Please include your device type, app version if available, what you expected to happen, what happened instead, and your in-app Support ID if the issue is related to purchases or free usage.

Restore purchases

If Whatifolio Unlimited is not active after reinstalling the app or switching devices, open the paywall and choose Restore purchase. Purchases are restored through the App Store or Google Play account used at checkout.

Manage subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed by Apple App Store or Google Play. You can cancel or manage a subscription in your store account settings. Deleting the app does not automatically cancel a subscription.

Free calculation limits

Free users receive a limited number of calculations each month. The app may also use network-based limits to reduce abuse. Paid Whatifolio Unlimited access removes calculation limits.

Calculation results

Whatifolio results are estimates for educational and entertainment purposes. They are not investment advice and should not be used as the only basis for financial decisions.
